Here I have Item Building to represent the design methodology of iterative design. The reason I chose Item Building to represent iterative design is because as you play the game, you build up items in iterations. You start with the most basic items and as you play the game you begin to build on top of those items, sometimes selling some items, in order for you to get stronger and better ones. Eventually, you will have the final items that will bring out the full potential of your champion. Below are a set of pictures that demonstrate the flow of a build for a particular item.

Here I have Ranks and Divisions of the game to represent the layering and seperation aspect of our curriculum. The Ranks of the game go as such: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, and Challenger. Within each of these ranks, there are divisions that go as such V, IV, III, II, I. Players are seperated into a certain division and rank in order to show their skill level. This also enables them to be placed against other players in their skill level so that each player gets a fair advantage when playing a ranked game.
